About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer (~Assistant Professor) at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge, UK. My research focuses on the intersection of trustworthy and responsible AI, privacy-preserving AI, safety and security of AI application, Internet of Things, and Cloud/Edge computing. Additionally, I engage in interdisciplinary research, utilizing AI for various applications such as healthcare, sustainable and smart cities, cybersecurity, and Software-defined Network (SDN).

Prior to joining ARU, I worked as a KTP Associate - Data Scientist at the University of Bristol. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Nevada, Reno under the supervision of Dr. Shamik Sengupta. I worked as Junior Research Fellow (JRF) at IIT Kanpur. I received my masters degree from NIT Kurukshetra and completed my B.Tech from BIET Jhansi.

I would be happy to consider applications from prospective PhD students. Visit department page Computing and Information Science PhD to know more about our PhD program.
