About Me

I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Nevada, Reno. I work under the supervision of Dr. Shamik Sengupta. Before joining UNR, I worked as a Junior Research Fellow (JRF) at IIT Kanpur. I received my masters degree from NIT Kurukshetra in year 2013 and completed my B.Tech from BIET Jhansi, in year 2011.

My research intersects in the following areas:

  • Cyber Security: Security Data Science, Anomaly Detection
  • Internet of Things: Plug-in Electric Vehicle Charging, Smart Grid, Cloud and Edge Computing, Sensor Deployement
  • Machine Learning: Deep Neural Network, Clustering, Predictive Analytics
