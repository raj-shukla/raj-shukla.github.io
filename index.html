About Me

I am a Lecturer (~ assistant professor in USA) at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge, UK. My research intersects with the Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Internet of Things. I am particularly interested in interdisciplinary research

Prior to joining ARU, I worked as a KTP Associate - Data Scientist at the University of Bristol. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Nevada, Reno under the supervision of Dr. Shamik Sengupta. I worked as Junior Research Fellow (JRF) at IIT Kanpur. I received my masters degree from NIT Kurukshetr and completed my B.Tech from BIET Jhansi.

I would be happy to consider applications from prospective PhD students. Visit department page Computing and Information Science PhD to know more about our PhD program.
