ABOUT ME


I am a KTP Associate - Data Scientist at the University of Bristol. I have experience in Data Science, Machine Leaning, NLP, Cyber Security, and IoT. My current project is to abridge the gap between academia and industry and bring the mutual benefits of both the domain. I deliver research project having long-term commercial impact to a SME Claritum as well as disseminating the inherent concepts to academia through research publications. 

Prior to this I completed my  Ph.D. in  Computer Science and Engineering at University of Nevada, Reno. I worked under the supervision of Dr. Shamik Sengupta. I worked as Junior Research Fellow (JRF) at IIT Kanpur. I received my masters degree from NIT Kurukshetra in year 2013 and completed my B.Tech from BIET Jhansi, in year 2011. Over the years, I have accrued the experience to write research grant proposals, manage and mentor students in their projects, and deliver class lectures.
